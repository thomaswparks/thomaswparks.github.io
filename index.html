    Curiosity drives my passion…


    From a young age, my curiosity led me to dismantle toys to understand their mechanics. This same curiosity now drives me to dissect databases, reverse engineer systems, and explore virtualization in my home lab. It has also pushed me to learn HTML, JavaScript, and CSS. My insatiable thirst for knowledge and the desire to create has propelled me to earn a degree in Computer Science, graduating at the top of my class.


    For the past 24 years, I’ve been deeply involved in electronics, radio frequency systems, and IT. However, I always felt something was missing. A few years ago, I identified that gap and embarked on a journey to overcome it, starting by earning my degree and graduating Summa Cum Laude. After this achievement, I continued to broaden my knowledge while completing my career in the U.S. Air Force, taking on roles such as Network Engineer, Innovation Leader, and IT Project Manager.


    Now, as I transition from my military career, I’m ready to take the final step in my journey. Contact me if you want to connect!
