    Curiosity drives my passion… Software Engineering

    Curiosity has taken me from smashing toys to see how they worked as a child, to dissecting databases so I can reverse engineer, fix, or re-tool them, to building a home lab so I can explore and understand virtualization. Curiosity has also driven me to learn HTML, JavaScript, and CSS. Curiosity is what propelled me to earn my degree in Computer Science, graduating in the top of my class. Curiosity is what brings me here today, that insatiable thirst for knowledge and understanding. The urge to build something new.

    For the past 24 years, I have been immersed in the electronics, radio frequency, and IT world, but there has always been something missing. A few years back I realized what that was and began my journey by smashing through that first obstacle, earning my degree, and graduating Summa Cum Laude. With that first obstacle behind me I had a lull in the action, but with my sights set on my end goal I finished out my USAF career by soaking up as much knowledge as possible through career broadening. I assumed roles as a Network Engineer, an Innovation Leader, and an IT Project Manager. Now, as I transition from my Air Force career, I’m taking that final step.
